Antoine Fourrier

Landscape Architect

Antoine Fourrier is a landscape architect with several years of experience. He currently works at REDscape and has collaborated with various companies at home and abroad, such as Paridon x de Groot, LODEWIJK BALJON, Planorama, Bureau B + B and Artgineering. He participated in the development of ambitious urban visions and in the design of inspiring public spaces. He also worked on design with regard to the public domain, master planning, urban design (squares, streets, park design) and its implementation (detailing, street furniture). At REDscape, Antoine has worked on various heritage sites, including UNESCO and national monuments. Antoine has experience with design research, design development and graphic communication. He participated on a regional scale in the development of research projects with strategy and landscape transformation. One of the most recent studies is "Foodscape" about food-productive spaces in metropolitan areas.
